About Our Salon

I have been a hairdresser for 16 years. I have the experience to service multi textured clientele thru years of service and continuing education in the hair care world. Staying connected to Seattles hairdressing community thru events and classes keeps us inspired and in top of our game to provide the best for all of the various needs our guests may have. I am fulfilled as a person when I uplift people through hair. Seeing and feeling my guests feeling beautiful and spirits full of joy after our service together is really why i do what I do! I want to help people embrace themselves and feel joyful!
